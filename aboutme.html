About Me


My name is Jessica Clarke. I am a senior at the University of Colorado Boulder studying Informatino Science with minors in Space and Computer Science.

I am the current President of Women of Aeronautics and Astronautics at CU. WoAA is a student organization dedicated to women and gender minorities to encourage those in and around engineering to broaden and explore their passion for space.

As a senior at CU Boulder, I have taken an unorthodox path to find my passion. In my first two years at CU I was a film studies major, which expanded my appreciation for cinema, music, and the arts. The summers following I worked at Rainbow Trail Lutheran Camp as their videographer and photographer. Making the dramatic change to information science, I discovered that I have a passion for computer science, coding, and space.

This summer I am appart of the CU in DC aborad program, and I will be interning at NASA Headquarters focusing on Natural Disaster Program Automation. As I will be living and working in the DC area I hope to learn and grow professionally and mentally.
