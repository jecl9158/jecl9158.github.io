Rick and Morty "For the boys"

While I am a fan of Rick and Morty the TV show, and I know is satire comdey, the representation of women is low. How do I know this? I watched the show, and also used the rick and morty api to complie a data set of 60 characters and divide up their genders. What I concluded from this is that, on average, there are more males in this show than females. Statisticly speaking. There is a greater chance that any character in this show is a male.

The data set I used for this argument comes from git hub, on the Rick and Morty API. I looked at 60 characters and compiled their genders. Here are the totals that I came up with;

  • Male: 43
  • Female: 12
  • Unknown: 5
